What BoardGauge does

Structured questionnaire library

SEBI LODR-aligned evaluation frameworks for the Board as a whole, individual Directors, and committees. Pre-built and fully customisable. Covers composition, process, dynamics, strategy, and independence.

Confidential digital response collection

Directors respond securely. Individual responses are not visible to peers, management, or the Company Secretary during the evaluation period. Confidentiality is technical, not policy-based.

Automated analysis and scoring

Aggregated results with benchmarking. Identifies governance gaps across Board composition, process, dynamics, and strategy. Trend analysis across years from the first evaluation.

NRC-ready reporting

Formatted output for the Nomination and Remuneration Committee meeting — produced automatically when the response window closes. No manual compilation. One export.

Annual Report disclosure support

Board evaluation summary formatted for the Annual Report disclosure required under SEBI LODR. Ready to paste into the Annual Report without reformatting.

Historical tracking

Multi-year trend data on board effectiveness. Governance progress is visible and reportable. The Chairman can identify structural governance issues before they become regulatory concerns.
