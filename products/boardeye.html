What BoardEye does

Secure board pack distribution

Encrypted delivery to directors with full version control. Replaces the "Board Pack v4 FINAL FINAL.pdf" problem permanently. Directors always access the current version only.

In-app director annotations

All director notes captured in-platform. Searchable, retained, and visible only to the annotating director unless shared. No scattered PDFs with handwritten notes.

Digital resolution management

Draft, circulate, vote on, and execute resolutions with a complete, timestamped audit trail. Every vote attributed, every amendment logged. Legally defensible from day one.

Attendance and quorum tracking

Automated quorum calculation and minutes-ready attendance records. No manual counting. Compliance with Companies Act 2013 Section 174 quorum requirements is enforced automatically.

Committee management

Separate secure workspaces for Audit, Risk, Nomination, and Remuneration committees. Tailored access permissions by committee. Separate pack and resolution workflows per committee.

Document retention

All board materials retained with tamper-evident log per statutory requirements under Companies Act 2013. Produces inspection-ready board records in minutes, not days.
