What InsiderLens IFCo does

Chinese Wall automation

Information barrier controls between deal teams, research, and trading. Barriers are logged and auditable — not just policy-declared. Every wall event is timestamped and attributed.

Intermediary SDD

Dedicated database for UPSI received from clients. Access logged by employee, deal, and timestamp. Separate from the listed-company SDD — built for the intermediary's specific obligation.

Deal-level UPSI tracking

Assign, monitor, and close UPSI access by deal. Know exactly who accessed what, and when. Access automatically terminates at deal closure or employee departure.

Employee DP monitoring

Covers all staff with UPSI access, not just listed-company Designated Persons. Required under the amended Regulation 9A scope. Triggered by role assignment, not manual lists.

Annual compliance workflow

Automated training dispatch, acknowledgement collection, and record retention. The full annual compliance cycle — policy distribution to sign-off — in one platform.

Regulatory reporting templates

Pre-built output formats for SEBI inspection documentation and internal audit packs. Ready to produce at inspection notice with zero manual compilation.
