What InsiderLens LCo does

UPSI identification engine

Flags information meeting the amended UPSI definition. Logs to the SDD automatically with timestamp and accessor record. Updated for the March 2025 broadened definition.

Structured Digital Database (SDD)

Cloud-hosted, access-controlled, tamper-evident. Produces audit-ready reports in two clicks. Meets Regulation 3(5) obligations including 8-year retention without manual archiving.

Trading window management

Automated open/close notifications to all Designated Persons with delivery receipts retained. No manual email coordination. Full log of every window event and acknowledgement.

Pre-clearance workflow

Digital request, review, approval, and rejection trail for DP trades. Replaces email threads and WhatsApp confirmations. Every decision timestamped and attributed.

₹10L disclosure alerts

Automated triggers for DP trades above the threshold. Exchange filing prompts within the 2-trading-day window. Zero manual monitoring required.

8-year retention

Configurable record archiving with tamper-evident audit log. Meets the full 8-year SDD retention requirement under Regulation 3(5) without additional storage infrastructure.
